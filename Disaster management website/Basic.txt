Man-made Disasters:

1)Industrial Accidents: The Bhopal gas tragedy in 1984 resulted in thousands of deaths and injuries due to a gas leak.
2)Oil Spills: The Deepwater Horizon oil spill in 2010 caused extensive environmental damage in the Gulf of Mexico.
3)Hazardous Material Incidents: The Chernobyl nuclear disaster in 1986 released radioactive material, causing a significant impact on human health and the environment.
4)Chemical Spills: The 2012 Costa Concordia disaster involved the cruise ship running aground and spilling fuel and chemicals into the ocean.
5)Nuclear Accidents: The Fukushima nuclear disaster in 2011 led to widespread contamination and evacuations in Japan.
6)Industrial Fires: The 2012 Ali Enterprises factory fire in Pakistan resulted in a large number of fatalities due to poor safety measures.
7)Terrorism: The September 11, 2001, terrorist attacks in the USA caused significant loss of life and infrastructure damage.
8)Cyber Attacks: Large-scale cyber attacks on critical infrastructure can disrupt services and cause economic harm.
